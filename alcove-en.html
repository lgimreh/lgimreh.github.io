alcøve

Conceptual exercise consisting in the creation of a heterotopic space inspired by a prompt written by Sophie Cadieux for the class. The space is meant as an externalization of the mental barriers the narrator erects within her psyche to protect from the abuse she suffers throughout her life. In this way, the space generates a safe haven, a nook, an alcove from which the narrator can take a step back from her struggles.
